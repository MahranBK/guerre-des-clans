Game Rules

Objective

Defeat all enemy units to claim victory for your clan!

Game Phases

  • Setup Phase: Place your units in your territory
  • Movement Phase: Move your units strategically
  • Action Phase: Attack enemies or use special abilities

Unit Types

  • Warrior: Strong melee fighters with high defense
  • Archer: Long-range attackers with high accuracy
  • Mage: Powerful spellcasters with area effects

Clan Bonuses

  • Mountain Clan: +2 Defense for all units
  • Plains Clan: +2 Accuracy for archers
  • Wise Clan: 1.5x Spell power for mages
